Summary

Several minutes after his conversation with transfer student Homura Akemi, Harry noticed a slight discrepancy in her goodbye. “Wait, did she say ‘fellow girl?’ What in the world???” As Harry mildly panicked over what it meant that someone he never interacted with was somehow under the impression that the ‘Boy who lived’ was a girl, a second, much more subtle thought suddenly struck his mind. “Wait, she called me a girl but I didn’t even notice until the conversation was over… what in the world is up with that?!?”

“What in Merlin’s name is going on!” he finally exclaimed.
